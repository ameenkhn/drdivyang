About Dr. Divyang Patel

Physician care rooted in ICU discipline and a patient-first approach.

Dr. Divyang Patel believes that effective healthcare begins with understanding the patient as a whole person. His consultations focus on careful listening, clear explanation, practical treatment plans, and preventive guidance so patients can make informed decisions about their health.

He currently works as a full-time consultant physician and intensivist at Gupta Hospital, Bilimora, and also serves as a visiting consultant in Gandevi and Chikhli, helping adult patients above 13 years of age with acute illness, chronic disease control, and indoor care when needed.
