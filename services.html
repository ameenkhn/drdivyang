Internal Medicine & Critical Care Services

Home Services

Medical care for adults above 13 years, from OPD consultation to indoor management.

Dr. Divyang Patel’s practice combines physician-led OPD care, indoor patient management, and critical care judgment for patients above 13 years of age. The focus is on accurate diagnosis, clear explanation, rational testing, and practical treatment that matches both the illness and the patient’s overall health context.

  • OPD consultations
  • Indoor patient facility
  • ECG & 2D-ECHO
  • X-ray & Laboratory
  • Physiotherapy support
  • Evidence-based treatment

Core clinical services

The service mix covers chronic disease control, acute infections, respiratory disease, stroke and CNS presentations, kidney disease, abdomen-related illness, and poisoning cases. Care plans are individualized, with strong emphasis on preventive advice and follow-up where needed.

Lifestyle Disease Management

Diabetes mellitus type 1 and 2, hypertension, thyroid disorder, and dyslipidemia.

Infections

Malaria, dengue, pneumonia, typhoid, and prolonged fever with monitoring and supportive care.

Respiratory Disease

Asthma, COPD, and acute respiratory complaints needing physician assessment and treatment.

CNS Cases

CV stroke (paralysis), meningitis, and urgent neurological evaluation.

Kidney & Abdomen Related Disease

Evaluation, investigation planning, and medical management for renal and abdominal conditions.

Poisoning & Critical Cases

Prompt stabilization, close observation, and intensive physician involvement when required.
